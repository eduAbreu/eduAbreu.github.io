The Ideal Host

Our ideal hosts are people who want to see international students thrive in different environments. We are looking for supportive, compassionate, open individuals who are willing to share their home and culture and learn from others. Our hosts are an extension of the HomestayMatch.com team and are essentially the face of the company. HomestayMatch.com wants you to feel apart of the team; we make every effort to support you through the entire hosting process. From applying to saying good bye to your students. So what are you waiting for? Join the team of people who help us do what we do.
