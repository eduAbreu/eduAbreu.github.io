Why Hosting a Student?

Share your culture, show your pride. With every great exchange there is an opportunity to learn new and exciting things while giving that same opportunity to someone else. Here at Homestay Match we believe that the students shouldn't be the only who gets a great experience, host shoudl as well, all while receiving a passive income!

Why Homestay Match?

Well this isn't our first rodeo, we've been hosting international students for 20 years now. We have successfuly placed thousands of students in caring homes across the United States, offering competitive pay. We work alongside some of the most prominent university's international programs.

No experience, no worries! We offer 24/7 guaranteed support. Our team never leaves your side. Homestay Match is a leader in hosting international students and we absolutely love what we get to do, and you can be a part of that.

Becoming a host

If you have a spare bedroom furnished with a bed, desk, and closet space, you're the right person for the job. In a few easy steps you can be on your way to providing hospitality, experience culture, and earning passive income, which is EPIC (Every Person's Ideal Compensation)! Here's how it works:

  • 1. Apply and get approved

    Once you have completed the short online application process, our team will schedule a house visit where we verify your location, check the home conditions, and approve the student's room.

  • 2. Build an awesome profile

    Your personal profile is your first impression you make as a host. Tell us about you and your space. And don't worry, we have a very simple guide to help you build the perfect profile.

  • 3. Meet and Match

    Your space is now visible on our website! Homestay Match students are constantly on the quest of finding housing. You can accept or deny booking requests, so you will only host students that fit your preferences.

  • 4. Enjoy, Experience, and Earn

    Once the approved student has settled in you will receive the payment! All that is left to do is enjoy each other's company, experience each other culture, and earn extra income. And before you know it you will be a master at hosting international students!
